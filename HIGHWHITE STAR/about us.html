About Us

High White Star Investments Ltd is a company specializing in soap production, chemical manufacturing for soap, and training individuals in soap-making skills. It provides quality products and empowers people through hands-on training, helping them gain practical knowledge in the industry.

Mission

Connecting Professionals with each other

Vision

To be the most reliable and trusted soap production and training company in Uganda

Objectives of the Organisation

    To produce high quality soaps and soap chemicals for both local and international markets

    To provide hands-on training in soap-making skills to individuals seeking to enter the soap industry

    To promote sustainable practices in soap production and chemical manufacturing

    To contribute to the growth and development of the local economy through job creation and skill development

Our Services

We offer a range of services including soap production, chemical manufacturing for soap, and training in soap-making skills. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering high-quality products and empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in the soap industry.

We also offer decent packaging and delivery to different areas of the country.
